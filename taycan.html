Taycan


Porsche Taycan is a battery electric saloon and shooting brake produced by German automobile manufacturer Porsche. The concept version of the Taycan, named the Porsche Mission E, debuted at the 2015 Frankfurt Motor Show.The Taycan was revealed fully production-ready at the 2019 Frankfurt Motor Show as Porsche's first series production electric car
